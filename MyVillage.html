Narasingole is a village in Zarugumalli mandal in Prakasam District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Andhra region.

It is located 32 KM towards south from District head quarters Ongole. 7 KM from Zarugumalli. 193 KM from state capital Amaravati
